Pindar Opera Viewer

Welcome to the Pindar Opera Viewer (POV), a Pindar Multitext or choose-your-own adventure into the epinikia of Pindar. The project began as an attempt to provide some updates and a translation to Anders Bjørn Drachmann's 3-volume Teubner edition of the Scholia Vetera, but picked a new way of thinking about textual criticism, largely inspired by the Homer Multitext project of Casey Dué and Mary Ebbott as well as many scholars of Meso-American and Medieval texts discussed in Chapter 1 of the dissertation.

Take a look at the documentation for tips and tricks, or jump right in!
